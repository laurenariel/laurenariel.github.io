THE GLOBAL CLIMATE STRIKE

On Friday, September 20th, 2019 over 4 million people around the world skipped school and work to participate in the Global Climate Strike. The students of New York marched from Foley Square to Battery Park, demanding their government bring them climate justice. Shot forEarther and Gizmodo Media Company.
