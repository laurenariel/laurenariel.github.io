DOWN TO PA: THE 2020 ELECTION

The 2020 Presidential Election captivated the nation for almost a week. Candidates President Donald Trump and former Vice President Joe Biden were neck in neck from November 2nd to 7th, with Biden being announced President-Elect around noon on the 7th. Pennsylvania, one of the battleground states, was the deciding vote. The following is a depiction of the response throughout the state that weekend. Shot November 7th and 8th, 2020, forThe New School Free Press.
